Privacy policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

1. Introduction

This privacy policy describes how Sidekick (the service operated at sidekick.amngupta.com and related deployments) collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use our application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling data responsibly.

2. Information we collect

A. Account and authentication

If you sign in with Google, we receive identifiers and profile information from Google’s OpenID Connect service, such as your Google subject ID (sub), email address, and name where provided. We use this to identify your session and scope data to your account.

B. OAuth tokens

To access Google services you authorize (for example Calendar, Tasks, and optionally Keep), we store OAuth tokens (including refresh tokens where issued) in our database so the service can act on your behalf until you revoke access or sign out. You can revoke the app’s access in your Google Account permissions.

C. Content you provide

We process messages you send to the assistant, including text you type in the chat. We also store tasks, schedule entries, and notes you create through the app in our database (for example PostgreSQL or AlloyDB), associated with your account identifier.

D. Google product data

When you use features that integrate with Google Calendar, Google Tasks, or Google Keep, we read and write data in those products according to the permissions you grant. That data is processed to fulfill your requests (for example listing or creating items).

E. Technical and usage data

Like most web services, our hosting infrastructure may log technical data such as IP addresses, request paths, timestamps, and error information for security, reliability, and debugging. Analytics or monitoring tools configured for the deployment may also process similar metadata.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the Sidekick service and its AI-assisted features.
  • To authenticate you, enforce access control, and associate stored data with your account.
  • To call Google APIs you have authorized, in line with the scopes shown at sign-in.
  • To improve reliability and fix issues (for example using logs and error reports).
  • To comply with law or protect rights and safety where required.

4. Artificial intelligence and model providers

Conversations and tool use may be sent to Google generative AI services (for example Gemini via Vertex AI or related Google AI infrastructure) to generate responses. Those providers process prompts and context under their terms and, where applicable, your Google Cloud configuration. Do not submit secrets or highly sensitive personal data in chat if you are uncomfortable with that processing.

5. Sharing your information

A. Service providers

We rely on third parties to run the service, including Google (sign-in, Google APIs, AI, and cloud infrastructure such as Cloud Run, AlloyDB, or Vertex AI as configured). Those providers process data as necessary to deliver their services.

B. Legal requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (for example a court or government agency).

C. Business transfers

If the service is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to appropriate notices where required.

6. Data security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data retention

We retain information only as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. You may request deletion of stored account-related data where applicable (subject to legal exceptions).

8. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data, or to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below. You may also use Google’s account tools to revoke app access and manage data held by Google.

9. Children’s privacy

The service is not directed at individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page and revise the “Last updated” date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy where permitted by law.

11. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact the operator of Sidekick at amngupta@protonmail.com.
